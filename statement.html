Statement of Faith

-We believe The Holy Scriptures are initially given by God, Divinely inspired, infallible, entirely trustworthy, and the supreme authority in all matters of Faith and Conduct.

-We believe in One God, eternally existent in three persons: Father, Son, and Holy Spirit -we believe Our Lord Jesus Christ, God manifested in the flesh, His virgin birth, His sinless human life, His mediatorial work, and His return in power and glory

-We believe in the Holy Spirit, by whose indwelling the believers are enabled to live a holy Life, to witness and for the Lord Jesus Christ.

-We believe in The Salvation of the lost and sinful man through the shed blood of the Lord Jesus Christ by Faith, apart from works and regeneration by The Holy Spirit.

-We believe in The Unit of the Spirit of all true believers, the Church, the body of Christ.

-We believe in the resurrection of both the saved and the lost; they are saved into the resurrection of life and lost into the resurrection of damnation.
