Our Leadership

Senior Pastor and Founder's word
Pst.Parfait Ntwari

**Meet Pastor Ntwari Parfait:** Ntwari Parfait is not just a Senior Lead Pastor; he's a visionary, a committed servant of God, and a beacon of hope within the Faith Family community. With a profound calling received at a young age, Pastor Parfait's journey has been one of dedication, growth, and service. At the tender age of 15, Pastor Parfait embraced his faith and embarked on a transformative journey, culminating in his baptism by Reverend Pr. Nsekanabo Jean Paul. His passion for ministry only deepened as he pursued his studies, establishing the Light of God prayer group during his time at College George Fox de Kagarama. His early leadership roles within the Fellowship set the stage for a life devoted to spreading the Gospel. Joining Zion Temple Celebration Center marked a pivotal moment in Pastor Parfait's spiritual journey, where he immersed himself in discipleship and service within the tribe of Benjamin Family. His subsequent involvement with Sons of Korah further fueled his mission to connect youth through biblical teachings, shaping him into the leader he is today. In 2021, Pastor Parfait answered God's call to establish Faith International Ministries and Faith Family Organization. Since then, he has led with unwavering dedication, drawing from his deep theological insights and pastoral care expertise honed during his pursuit of a Bachelor's degree in Religious Studies at Africa College of Theology. Pastor Parfait's leadership isn't confined to the pulpit; it extends to community outreach and support. As the Founder of Faith Family N.G.O., he orchestrates outreach programs, exemplifying his genuine concern for the less fortunate and earning the respect and admiration of all who know him. Despite the challenges inherent in ministry, Pastor Parfait remains steadfast in his commitment to serve. Whether investing countless hours in sermon preparation, pastoral counseling, or community engagement, his dedication never wavers. In July 2024, Pastor Parfait joyously welcomed another chapter in his life as he married his beloved Yfray Isimbi. Together, they epitomize a partnership grounded in faith and service, working hand in hand to uplift and inspire those around them. Pastor Ntwari Parfait's journey is a testament to the transformative power of faith and the enduring impact of servant leadership. As he continues to guide and inspire, his legacy of love, compassion, and unwavering dedication shines brightly, illuminating the path for all who seek solace and salvation.
